Girls Not Brides

Helping to bring awareness for girls in need

Although India has accepted a plan to reduce rates of child marriage, they have only been slowly decreasing. If we don’t hurry, the impact could be drastically felt all around the world by 2050. The majority of young girls who are married are under the age of 15. The main reason for this is because of their weak economic status. This website aims to help raise money to help prevent more marriages like this from happening and secure a girl’s chance of living a happy life.
